infoAbout

Tool-based Auto Smelt — equip smelting tools and let blocks drop their smelted forms automatically.

starKey Features

  • Craftable "smelting" variants of pickaxe, axe, shovel
  • Equipped smelting tool auto-smelts mined blocks
  • Works with all smeltable blocks and ores
  • Fortune equivalent bonus on smelted drops
  • Tools have standard durability
  • Per-tool control — mix and match as needed
