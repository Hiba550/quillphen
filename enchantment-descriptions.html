Enchantment Descriptions

Detailed enchantment tooltips for better decision making while enchanting

Both Editions Resource Pack Most Popular

Description

This resource pack enhances Minecraft's enchantment system by providing detailed descriptions of every enchantment directly in-game. Whether you're new or experienced, it helps you make better choices while enchanting your gear.

Features

  • Detailed descriptions for all vanilla enchantments
  • Shows enchantment effects and mechanics
  • Includes maximum levels and compatibility information
  • Available for both Java and Bedrock editions
  • No more guessing what each enchantment does
  • Perfect for new and experienced players

What's Included

  • Weapon enchantments (Sharpness, Knockback, Fire Aspect, etc.)
  • Tool enchantments (Efficiency, Fortune, Silk Touch, etc.)
  • Armor enchantments (Protection, Thorns, Feather Falling, etc.)
  • Bow enchantments (Power, Punch, Flame, Infinity)
  • Special enchantments (Mending, Unbreaking, Curse of Binding, etc.)
