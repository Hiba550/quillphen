Description

Easily spot mobs around you, even through walls. This pack highlights all nearby mobs within a 25-block radius with a glowing effect, making it perfect for exploring caves, preparing for combat, or simply staying aware of your surroundings.

Features

  • 25-Block Radius Detection: Highlights all mobs within a 25-block radius
  • See Through Walls: Glowing effect works through blocks and walls
  • All Non-Player Mobs: Affects hostile, neutral, and passive mobs
  • Real-time Updates: Automatically updates as mobs move in and out of range
  • Works with Empty Hands: No need to hold specific items
  • Multiplayer Compatible: Works perfectly on servers

How It Works

  • Applies the glowing effect to all nearby non-player mobs
  • Continuously scans a 25-block radius around you
  • Updates automatically in real-time as you move
  • Works whether holding an item or with empty hands
  • No configuration required - works immediately

Perfect For

  • Cave Exploration: Spot hidden mobs in dark caves
  • Combat Preparation: See threats before they see you
  • Base Security: Monitor for hostile mobs around your base
  • Mob Farming: Easily locate and track mobs for farms
  • Survival Safety: Stay aware of nearby dangers
  • Building Projects: Clear areas of mobs before construction

Compatibility

  • ✅ Minecraft Java Edition 1.17 and above
  • ✅ Works in both singleplayer and multiplayer
  • ✅ Compatible with other data packs
  • ✅ No client-side mods required
  • ✅ Server-friendly implementation

Installation

  1. Download the data pack from CurseForge
  2. Place the .zip file in your world's datapacks folder
  3. Use /reload command or restart the world
  4. Start exploring with enhanced mob vision!

Technical Details

  • Lightweight data pack with minimal performance impact
  • Uses vanilla glowing effect for compatibility
  • Automatic cleanup when mobs move out of range
  • No persistent data storage required
