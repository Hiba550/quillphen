Insight HUD

Lightweight mod that enhances your Minecraft experience by displaying essential data in a clean, minimalist overlay

Java Edition 391+ Downloads

Description

Insight HUD is a lightweight mod that enhances your Minecraft experience by displaying essential data in a clean, minimalist overlay. Perfect for players who want quick access to important information without cluttering their screen.

Key Features

  • Gear & Item Insight: Shows durability and key details of worn or held items in real-time
  • Combat HUD: While engaged in battle, tracks your target's health and displays its loot table
  • Minimalist Design: Clean interface that doesn't interfere with gameplay
  • Real-time Updates: All information updates dynamically as you play
  • Lightweight Performance: Optimized for minimal impact on game performance

Configuration

  • Press H (default keybind) to open the Insight HUD Settings
  • Toggle HUD modules like Gear Info or Entity Info
  • Customize which information is displayed
  • Adjust positioning and appearance

Perfect For

  • Players who want to monitor equipment durability
  • Combat enthusiasts who need target information
  • Anyone seeking a cleaner, more informative HUD
  • Streamers and content creators
  • Survival players who need quick item insights

Installation

  1. Download the mod from CurseForge
  2. Install with your preferred mod loader (Fabric/Forge/NeoForge)
  3. Launch Minecraft and press H to configure
  4. Customize settings to your preference

  • Fully configurable interface
