Coordinates HUD

Display your coordinates on screen with a clean, customizable HUD overlay

Java Edition Mod

Description

Coordinates HUD is a mod that provides a clean and customizable way to display your current position coordinates on screen. Perfect for builders, explorers, and anyone who needs to keep track of their location in Minecraft.

Features

  • Clean, minimal HUD display
  • Customizable position and appearance
  • Shows X, Y, Z coordinates
  • Lightweight and performance-friendly
  • Easy to toggle on/off
  • Compatible with other HUD mods

Perfect For

  • Building massive structures
  • Exploring caves and underground areas
  • Keeping track of favorite locations
  • Navigation without opening debug screen
