Smithing Templates Descriptions

Detailed descriptions for smithing templates with usage and lore information

Both Editions Utility

Description

This mod/addon replaces smithing template item names with detailed, multi-line descriptions including usage, duplication, and lore information. It enhances in-game clarity and immersion beyond what resource packs can achieve.

Features

  • Detailed descriptions for all smithing templates
  • Includes usage instructions
  • Shows duplication methods
  • Adds lore and background information
  • Enhanced in-game clarity
  • Better immersion than basic resource packs

Supported Templates

  • Netherite Upgrade Template
  • All Armor Trim Templates (Coast, Dune, Eye, Host, etc.)
  • Complete formatting and descriptions
  • Historical and crafting context
