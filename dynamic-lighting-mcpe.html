Description

Add real-time lighting to Minecraft Bedrock! Light up your world by just holding torches, lanterns, or glowstone - no placing required! This lightweight addon transforms your exploration experience with dynamic lighting that follows you wherever you go.

Features

  • Real-time dynamic lighting system
  • Works by simply holding light sources
  • Achievements will not be disabled
  • Compatible with Actions & Stuffs
  • Compatible with Vibrant Visuals
  • Lightweight and performance-optimized
  • Works in both main hand and offhand
  • Underwater lighting support

Supported Light Items

  • Torch - Light Level 15
  • Lantern - Light Level 15 (Waterproof)
  • Soul Torch - Light Level 10
  • Soul Lantern - Light Level 10 (Waterproof)
  • Redstone Torch - Light Level 7
  • Lava Bucket - Light Level 15
  • Glowstone - Light Level 15 (Waterproof)
  • End Rod - Light Level 15 (Waterproof)
  • Campfire - Light Level 15
  • Soul Campfire - Light Level 10

How It Works

  • Checks hands every 0.15s (3 ticks) for light sources
  • Spawns invisible light_block at your position
  • Removes lights as you move or unequip items
  • Detects water blocks for underwater items
  • Restores original blocks (air/water) afterward

Installation

  1. Download the addon from CurseForge
  2. Enable both Resource Pack and Behavior Pack in world settings
  3. Hold a light source and enjoy dynamic lighting!

Important Notes

  • ✅ Achievements will not be disabled
  • ✅ Works with Actions & Stuffs addon
  • ✅ Works with Vibrant Visuals addon
  • ❌ Not compatible with Ray Tracing
